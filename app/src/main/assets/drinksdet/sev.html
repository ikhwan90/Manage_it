The Melon Detox Water

This awesome slimming detox recipe will do wonders for your body. It's tasty and refreshing and has tons of health benefits such as boosting the digestive track and helping your immune system to perform better. The best time to consume this belly slimming detox drink is in the morning.

 

Ingredients 

  • 3 Thin slices of orange
  • 2 Mint leaves shredded
  • 3 Thins slices of lemon
  • Water

How To Make It 

  • Get a clean glass jar and fill it with 3/4 of ice
  • Add the water, fruit and mint.
  • Stir for about 1 minute
  • Call for the jar and let it infuse for about 15 minutes.
  • Serve chilled.
