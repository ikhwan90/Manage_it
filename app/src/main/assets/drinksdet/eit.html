Green Tea Detox Drink

As the name suggests, this recipe is perfect for anyone who is trying to shed some pounds. By now you should know the power of green tea when it comes on to burning fat, especially when it is combined with other natural ingredients.

 

Ingredients 

  • 1/4 Ice water
  • 3 Slices of lime
  • Half cup of green tea (cold)
  • Ice
  • Chopped up mint

How To Make It 

  • Place all the ingredients in a clean glass jar and put it in the refrigerator overnight.
  • Serve with ice and enjoy.
