The Cellulite Juice

This amazing drink will definitely improve your life.

Hydrating your body with this nutritional drink will do benefits not only for weight loss, but also your skin, hair, eyes, nails and even your emotional thoughts.

But it also does a fantastic job at eliminating visible cellulite. It has anti-cancer properties as it contains high levels of vitamins C and A.

 

Ingredients 

  • 1 Lemon
  • 2 Limes
  • 1/4 Medium Pineapple
  • 5 Grapefruits
  • Small handful of ginger

How To Make It 

  • Peel the grapefruits but do not cut the white part away as it contains tons of nutrients.
  • Do the same for the lemon, pineapple and limes.
  • Now you will juice the grapefruit, lime, ginger and lemon. Juice the pineapple last.
  • After juicing, pour it over some ice drink and enjoy.
